WHY CHOOSE US

AFFORDABLE PAYMENTS


Cost

Just as all of us are different, the orthodontic issues we have are also different. This means that our fees depend on the difficulty of your treatment, appliances required and time in treatment.

When we determine your orthodontic care plan we can discuss the financial plan that works for you.



Payment Plans

Orthodontic treatment is an investment that will benefit you for a lifetime. Because we believe finances shouldn’t get in the way of achieving a healthy, beautiful smile, we offer a variety of payment options.

These options include a discount for payment in full prior to starting treatment as well as a variety of interest-free and no-down payment options.

For your added convenience, we accept all major credit cards and can set up automatic deductions.

We are more than willing to work with you to achieve the beautiful, healthy smile you deserve.



 

Individualized Treatments Plans

Your initial appointment will consist of a thorough examination and a discussion of potential treatment options. We may also reserve time following the exam to take some diagnostic records. The records may include X-rays, photos and impressions for study models that we need so we can create your customized treatment plan. This will last approximately one hour.

This important one-hour visit will give us insight into your orthodontic needs. We know your time is valuable.



Next Steps

We believe it’s important to analyse specific needs of each patient because in-depth planning leads to superior results and higher patient satisfaction. We will then schedule a consultation visit to discuss treatment options, time frames and financial arrangements.

We insist that our patients leave the office with a clear understanding of their specific needs, what the treatment will consist of and how long it will take. Also, we will answer any additional questions.

Then you’ll choose the type of braces you want—traditional metal, clear braces, or Invisalign.

Our goal at Faces with Braces is not just to give you the smile of your dreams. We want you to leave our office after every appointment knowing that you got the best possible care

Personal Customer Service

From the moment you contact our office to your final retainer check appointment, our goal at Faces with Braces is your complete satisfaction.

After the initial examination and taking of diagnostic records, Dr.Cynthia will review your photos and X-rays to find the best way to treat your individual needs followed by a discussion of potential treatment options



Bring These Things

Please assist us by providing the following information at the time of your first visit:
1. Any panoramic X-ray taken within the past six months.
2. Any prior dental treatment and corresponding records.



Start Your Treatment the Same Day

Your orthodontic treatment journey involves a series of orthodontic visits, but we value the relationship that we will build with you and your family throughout that process. We want you to feel confident you’re receiving the best orthodontic care.

We value your time, and we know you want to make the most of your visit. Once you feel like we've answered all of your questions and concerns, you can start your treatment that same day!
